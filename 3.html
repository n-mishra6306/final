Responsibilities

This role offers genuine brand-building exposure, not just design tasks. You'll be working directly with the founder to shape the visual identity of our startup.

  • Design content assets for platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Threads
  • Create visuals using Canva/Figma—reels, carousels, static posts
  • Write engaging captions and assist with post scheduling
  • Co-develop visual storytelling under guidance from the founder
  • Work with developers on UI screenshots, product visuals, and designs

Required Skills

Canva/Figma/Adobe tools Social media design Visual communication Creative branding Social media trends CRM tools knowledge

Eligibility

  • Available to work on-site in Thane/Mumbai/Navi Mumbai
  • Able to commit 3–4 hours daily across a 3-month period
  • Passionate, proactive, and comfortable with feedback
  • Women starting or restarting their careers are especially encouraged to apply
