Overview

Amazon Prime Video is seeking a talented and passionate Senior Art Director to craft world-class marketing campaigns for its expanding slate of original programming. You'll collaborate with creative, project management, campaign, and production teams to define the unique visual identity for major titles. The work spans key art, OOH (Out-of-Home), digital, social, partnerships, and more.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Create Key Art guided by a Creative Director and adapt it for a 360° campaign approach across OOH, social, and digital
  • Contribute strategic thinking to ideation and comping stages
  • Guide and give feedback to visual designers
  • Develop key art and/or motion concepts (knowledge in both is a plus)
  • Lead visual design from brainstorming through delivery
  • Design high-quality visuals for TV, film, and brand campaigns
  • Incorporate feedback from Creative Directors and stakeholders
  • Use customer insights and data to inform creative decisions
  • Stay up to date with design and technology trends
  • Direct talent and photographers during photoshoots

Basic Qualifications

5+ years creative strategy experience Typography & design principles Adobe Creative Suite Keynote & Figma Concept to delivery leadership Portfolio focused on Key Art

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience collaborating with product, consumer research, and marketing teams
  • UI/UX experience
  • Experience managing creative teams and external partners
  • Ability to present creative effectively to senior executives
  • 8+ years of creative strategy and execution experience
  • Strategic thinker with strong tactical execution skills
  • Background in entertainment, media, or digital marketing
