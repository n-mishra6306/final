UI/UX Designer Intern

Andheri East, Mumbai, India • Full-time

The Student Researcher Program fosters academic collaboration by placing students on research projects aligned with Google's scientific and technological goals. You may be considered for projects with Google Research teams—including DeepMind, Google Cloud, among others. These are exploratory, innovation-driven projects that tackle real-world, large-scale problems.

Requirements:

  • Collaborate with the design team to craft intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces
  • Assist with UX strategies for both web and mobile products
  • Design and prototype product features using Figma and Framer
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to gather feedback and iterate designs
  • Convert Figma designs into interactive, high-fidelity prototypes using Framer
  • Work closely with developers to ensure seamless implementation of design elements
  • Keep up with UI/UX trends, tools, and best practices, while taking ownership of assigned projects
