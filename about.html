About InterCareer

Bridging the gap between talented individuals and their dream careers through daily internship and job opportunities.

Our Story

The Inspiration

Seeing the struggle students faced finding quality internships and graduates searching for their first jobs, we knew there had to be a better way.

The Launch

In 2020, we launched InterCareer with a simple mission: connect talent with opportunity through a streamlined, daily-updated platform.

The Growth

From a small startup to helping thousands find their dream roles, we've grown but never lost sight of our core values.

The Future

We're expanding our services to include career coaching, skill development, and personalized job matching.
